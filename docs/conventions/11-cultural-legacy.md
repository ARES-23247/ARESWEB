# Cultural Legacy

> FIRST core values and ARES 23247 engineering ethos. Read when writing team copy, documentation, or About content.

## FIRST Core Values

- **Discovery** — Explore new skills and ideas
- **Innovation** — Creativity and persistence to solve problems
- **Impact** — Apply learning to improve the world
- **Inclusion** — Respect differences
- **Teamwork** — Stronger together
- **Fun** — Celebrate what we do

## ARES Ethos

- **Motto:** "Be the Inspiration"
- **Mountaineer Mindset:** Grit, Determination, Innovation (from MARS 2614)
- **Core Vehicle Philosophy:** "The robots are the vehicle; the students are the cargo."

## FTC Awards Alignment

Align content to judged award criteria:
- **Inspire** — Ambassador for FIRST, outreach impact
- **Think** — Engineering portfolio, design journey
- **Connect** — STEM community, business plans
- **Sustain** — Fundraising, long-term plans
- **Innovate** — Creative engineering solutions

## Tone

- Be humble, not arrogant
- Weave values naturally into headers, intros, footers
- Celebrate to inspire, not to brag
