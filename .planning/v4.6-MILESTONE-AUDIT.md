## Lessons Learned (Hotfixes)

1. **Catch-all middleware for background work is dangerous in Hono.** The `apiRouter.use('*')` pattern blocked all API responses. Handler-level hooks are the correct pattern.
2. **Static imports of AI modules crash the worker.** Any module under `routes/ai/` that touches Workers AI or Vectorize bindings must use dynamic `import()` to stay out of the startup module graph.
